# Post-MVP Ideas & Enhancements

This document tracks all new ideas, potential features, and improvements that are identified during the current development sprints but are planned to be addressed **after** we reach our Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

---

## 1. Tailored Visualization Mechanism

**Context:**  
The current visualization builder might be slightly unclear or difficult to navigate for certain types of users (e.g., non-technical users or beginners).

**Proposed Idea:**  
Redesign or adapt the visualization mechanism to be tailored to the specific user's needs or skill level. 

**Action Plan (Post-MVP):**
- **User Profiling:** Identify the common types of users (personas) interacting with the visualization builder.
- **Adaptive UI:** Implement an interface that offers simplified, guided flows for beginners while keeping advanced configuration options available for power users.
- **Improved Guidance:** Add clearer tooltips, step-by-step wizards, or recommended charts based on the selected data types to make visualization generation more intuitive.

**Status:** `To Do` (Post-MVP)
